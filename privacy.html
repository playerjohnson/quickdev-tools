Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 February 2026

What Data We Collect

Tool data: All tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data you enter — JSON, regex patterns, text, colours — is ever sent to our servers or any third party. Everything is processed client-side.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymised usage data: pages visited, session duration, device type, and approximate location (country/city level). This helps us understand which tools are used most and improve the site.

Advertising: We display ads through Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage ad personalisation at Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

We use first-party analytics cookies (GA4) and third-party advertising cookies (Google AdSense). You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, object to processing, and withdraw consent. Since our tools process no personal data server-side, these rights primarily apply to analytics and advertising cookies.
